About

Hello! My name is Margo, and I’m the creator behind Dreamy Cake Shop. As a baker, I’ve always had a deep appreciation for the role that decorations and tools play in transforming baked goods into edible masterpieces. When I couldn’t find affordable, high-quality tools online, I decided to take matters into my own hands and start a 3D printing studio.

My small studio specializes in designing and creating custom stamps, cookie cutters, cake toppers, and other tools for baking enthusiasts. With 3D printing technology, I can bring any idea to life — whether it’s my own vision or a unique request from a client. This flexibility allows me to cater to both professional bakers and hobbyists alike.

Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of completing more than 1,500 successful sales, and I’m proud to say that every single customer has been 100% satisfied. The glowing reviews from real clients are a testament to the quality, creativity, and care that go into every product I make.

At Dreamy Cake Shop, I believe in empowering bakers with the tools they need to bring their creative visions to life. Whether you’re looking for a custom design or a practical solution for your baking needs, I’m here to help make your ideas a reality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are you?

I’m a baker who expanded into creating 3D-printed tools for baking enthusiasts. My products include stamps, cutters, toppers, and other custom baking accessories.

How can I place an order?

Simply select the item you want and click the "Buy" button, which will take you to my eBay store for a secure purchase.

Why eBay? Can I buy directly from your site?

I use eBay as it’s a transparent and safe platform with verified reviews. Direct purchases via my site aren’t available, but you can email me to discuss alternative arrangements.

What materials are your products made from?

Most of my items are made from food-safe PLA plastic, which is biodegradable and safe for use with edible goods.

Can I request a custom design?

Absolutely! I specialize in creating custom designs, including logos, fonts, and unique 3D-printed tools tailored to your needs.

How should I care for the products?

Wash gently with warm water and mild soap. Avoid using dishwashers, as the heat may damage the material.

Do you accept returns?

Returns are not accepted unless the item is defective or damaged during delivery. Please contact me if there are any issues.
